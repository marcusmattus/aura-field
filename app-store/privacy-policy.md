# Privacy Policy for chakraOS

**Last updated: July 10, 2026**

## Introduction

chakraOS ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application chakraOS (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Email address, display name, profile photo (if provided through social sign-in)
- **Authentication Data**: Encrypted passwords, social sign-in tokens (Google, Apple)
- **Profile Data**: Meditation preferences, focus areas, experience level, wellness goals

### Usage Information
- **Meditation Sessions**: Frequency preferences, session duration, completion rates
- **Journal Entries**: Text and voice notes (stored locally on your device)
- **Progress Data**: XP points, achievement milestones, streak counters
- **App Analytics**: Feature usage, crash reports, performance metrics (anonymized)

### Device Information
- **Device Identifiers**: App installation ID, device type, operating system version
- **Audio Data**: Microphone access for voice journal entries (processed locally)
- **Location Data**: General location for contextual meditation suggestions (optional)

## How We Use Your Information

### Core App Functionality
- Authenticate your account and maintain your session
- Sync your meditation progress across devices
- Generate personalized frequency recommendations
- Track your wellness journey and achievements

### Personalization
- Customize meditation experiences based on your preferences
- Provide AI-powered coaching insights
- Suggest optimal meditation frequencies and durations
- Send relevant reminders and encouragements

### Communications
- Send account-related notifications (verification, password reset)
- Deliver weekly insights and progress summaries (optional)
- Share feature updates and meditation tips (optional)
- Provide customer support when requested

### Analytics and Improvement
- Analyze app usage patterns to improve user experience
- Monitor app performance and fix technical issues
- Conduct research to enhance meditation effectiveness
- Generate anonymized insights for feature development

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- **Journal entries** are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers
- **Sensitive data** is encrypted using industry-standard encryption
- **Biometric data** (if any) remains on your device only

### Cloud Storage
- Account information and progress data are stored securely on Firebase
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Multi-factor authentication for administrative access

### Data Retention
- Active account data: Retained while your account is active
- Inactive accounts: Data deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- Analytics data: Anonymized and retained for up to 3 years
- Journal entries: Remain on your device until you delete the app

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

### Limited Sharing
- **Service Providers**: Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage
- **Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our legal rights
- **Business Transfer**: In the event of merger or acquisition (with user notification)

### Third-Party Services
- **Firebase Authentication**: For secure account management
- **Google Sign-In**: For optional social authentication
- **Apple Sign-In**: For optional social authentication (iOS)
- **Analytics Services**: For anonymized usage insights

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control
- **View** all personal information associated with your account
- **Edit** your profile, preferences, and meditation goals
- **Export** your meditation progress and achievement data
- **Delete** your account and associated data at any time

### Communication Preferences
- **Opt out** of promotional emails while keeping account notifications
- **Customize** which types of insights and updates you receive
- **Unsubscribe** from all communications except essential account updates

### Data Portability
- **Export** your data in a machine-readable format
- **Transfer** your information to another service (upon request)
- **Backup** your local journal entries using device backup features

## Children's Privacy

chakraOS is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

## International Users

If you are using chakraOS from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located and our central database is operated.

### GDPR Rights (European Users)
- **Right to Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your personal data
- **Right to Portability**: Receive your data in a structured format
- **Right to Object**: Opt out of certain data processing activities

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

- **Right to Know**: What personal information we collect and how it's used
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Opt out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

## Cookies and Tracking

### We Use Minimal Tracking
- **Essential cookies** for authentication and app functionality
- **Analytics cookies** for anonymized usage statistics (optional)
- **No advertising cookies** or behavioral tracking for ads
- **No cross-site tracking** or third-party data brokers

### Your Choices
- Disable analytics through app settings
- Use private browsing mode (web version)
- Adjust device-level tracking preferences
